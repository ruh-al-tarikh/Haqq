# About HAQ

**Discover forgotten Islamic history, Quranic mysteries, and prophetic storytelling.**

HAQ is a digital archive and storytelling project dedicated to uncovering the rich, often overlooked chapters of Islamic history. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and modern storytelling, providing high-quality visual content that educates and inspires.

From the mysteries of the Quran to the lives of the prophets and the great leaders of the past, HAQ brings history to life.
