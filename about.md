  <section class="mission-section">
    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>HAQ (Truth) is a digital archive and storytelling project dedicated to uncovering the rich, often overlooked chapters of Islamic history. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and modern storytelling, providing high-quality visual content that educates, inspires, and restores the sense of wonder in our heritage.</p>

    <p>We believe that history is not just a collection of dates and names, but a living tapestry of lessons for the soul. By exploring forgotten narratives and Quranic mysteries, we aim to provide a deeper understanding of our faith and the world around us.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="what-we-do">
    <h2>What We Explore</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Forgotten Chronicles:</strong> Stories of prophets, companions, and great leaders that aren't often heard.</li>
      <li><strong>Quranic Mysteries:</strong> Deep dives into the historical and scientific signs within the Holy Quran.</li>
      <li><strong>Signs of the Hour:</strong> Documenting and explaining the authentic signs of the End Times as described in our tradition.</li>
      <li><strong>Heritage & Culture:</strong> Celebrating the vast and diverse legacy of the Islamic world across continents and centuries.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="team-section">
    <h2>The Vision</h2>
    <p>HAQ started as a passion project to make Islamic history more accessible to the digital generation. In an era of rapid information, we strive to be a beacon of well-researched, visually compelling, and spiritually grounded content. We believe that by understanding our past, we can better navigate our future with clarity and purpose.</p>
  </section>
